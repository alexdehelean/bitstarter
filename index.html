DiaFree is a concept address to solve the problem of million on people with diabetes. Is dedicated to improve the health2 of people at risk with diabetes. Each year, thousands of people die from diabetes and many more suffer disabling complications, such as heart disease, kidney disease, blindness, and amputations. Diabetes patients are obliged to have drastic life change. Management diabetes concentrates on keeping blood sugar levels as close to normal ("euglycemia") as possible, without causing hypoglycemia. This can usually be accomplished with diet, exercise, and use of appropriate insulin in the case of type 1 diabetes.

Patient education, understanding, and participation is vital to keep diabetes under control. The goal of treatment is an HbA1C level of 6.5%, but should not be lower than that, and may be set higher. Attention is also paid to other health problems that may accelerate the deleterious effects of diabetes. These include smoking, elevated cholesterol levels, obesity, high blood pressure, and lack of regular exercise.
An usual day for people with type 1 diabetes includes:

  • monitor blood glucose levels several times per day
  • appropriately administer insulin pens whenever needed
  • work closely with healthcare team to determine which insulin are best for the patient

DiaFree wants to help people with diabetes to forget about all the daily problems and live a normal live, forgetting about daily blood checks and insulin shoots.
The DiaFree concept is composed of 3 major components:

  • Implanted blood glucose sensor
  • Insulin device holding application to automatically control sugar level
  • Personal mobile device application that communicates with the implanted device and internet

DiaFree concept refers to development of integrated implantable sensor and a insulin control device. Module will be controlled by software that can run autonomous based on parameters configuration. The concept defines external application that can be installed on personal mobile devices. These application will have multiple features integrated:

  • Collecting the data from the implanted device
  • Monitor the insulin level and warn the user when a threshold is reached
  • Monitor the correct functionality of the implanted device
  • Connect via internet to a online service and report the measured data to a selected physician
  • Configure parameters of the implanted device base on the physician recommendation. With patient agreement, remote tuning of the device by a physician should be made possible, in this way patient will only be informed about a change.

Basic work principles of DiaFree:

  • A implanted sensor read the blood glucose level at a configured interval monitor blood glucose levels several times per day
  • The processing module will check that glucose level are within configured thresholds. If the limit is passed, it will command a release of insulin. The quantity of insulin will be calculated based a algorithm that can be individually tuned based on patient.
  • The processing module is also responsible to maintain logs of the glucose level and insulin release. Using the connectivity module, external application will connect to the device and read the logs and parameter configuration.
  • Mobile device app will be configured to report the readings to a selected physician over the internet. Physician will be able to monitor the patients remotely and take decision related to patient conditions.
  • Based on the reported data, physician can indicate a parameter change in 2 modes explain above

DiaFree key points:

  • NO daily sugar test or insulin shoots
  • NO visit to physician necessary. Monitoring and corrective action can be done remotely
  • Better glucose control with insulin automatically being released when necessary-> reduce the risk diabetes complications
  • Better glucose monitoring as logs will be automatically sent via internet
  • Better life quality for the diabetes patients
